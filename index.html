    Frequently Asked Questions?

    This section has common questions that comes in your mind related to IPO.

    How to Subscribe to an IPO?
    +
    • Step 1: Login to your respective service provider.
    • Step 2: Click on the IPO section.
    • Step 3: Select the IPO you want to bid and enter the relevant details.
    • Step 4: Once the form is completed, place you make the payment or give permission.
    Should I buy an IPO first day?
    +

    It depends on various factors including company fundamentals, market conditions, and your investment goals.

    What is the minimum amount to invest in IPO?
    +

    The minimum investment amount depends on the lot size and price band of the specific IPO. Generally, it ranges from ₹10,000 to ₹15,000.

    When will IPO shares be credited to my account?
    +

    IPO shares are typically credited to your demat account within 6-8 working days after the issue closes, subject to allotment.
